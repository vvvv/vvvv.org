In this tutorial Christoph Ignaz Kirmaier aka [chk](https://vvvv.org/people/chk) of [3e8](https://3e8.studio/) shows you how to build a generative droplet spray. You'll create a custom datatype to dynamically spawn and animate instances of droplets with their own lifetime and behavior, making this tutorial a perfect entry point into object-oriented patching in VL.
