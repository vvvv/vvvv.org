First things first!

**vvvv gamma 7.2** is out. for details, please head over to the [change-log](https://thegraybook.vvvv.org/changelog/7.x.html).

We had another grande online meetup. If you haven't yet, please [rewatch it here](https://www.youtube.com/live/5_0ZN-0IocM)!

What next you ask? Here's the plan: We're hoping to soon™ have an 8.0 preview out, running on .NET 10 and including Boygrouping and the Package Manager. And also the promised laser work is making good progress, you can already undust your devices...

## vvvv in the news
- Berlin/Vienna based studio [3e8](/business/3e8) got featured by [interactive scape](https://www.interactive-scape.com) in a blog-post for the [custom multitouch experiences they build with vvvv and Scape X®](https://www.interactive-scape.com/blog/from-r-d-to-reality-how-3e8-builds-custom-multitouch-experiences-with-vvvv-and-scape-x).

- [Dataland](http://dataland.art) - The latest project by [Refik Anadol Studio](https://refikanadolstudio.com/), which we've been happy to consult on and develop many open-source packages for, is set to open on June 20 in Downtown LA. Almost every pixel on display in the space is either generated by or playing through vvvv. If you have the chance, [check it out](https://dataland.art/tickets), it is quite something!
