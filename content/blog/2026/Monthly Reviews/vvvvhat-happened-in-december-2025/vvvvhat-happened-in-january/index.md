January is done!

So what have you missed?
- We've released an [online browser for VL Packs](https://vvvv.org/blog/2026/introducing-an-online-browser-for-vl-packs/) and demoed the upcoming integrated package manager at the last meetup
- Here you can rewatch the [32nd vvvvorldwide meetup](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=7RdgcqFrmcY)
- There is an interesting job-posting up for creative-coders based in Los Angeles: https://dataland.art/careers/creative-coder
- We have work going on at various corners of vvvv that we hope to reveal in next months review...

Also of note to all shader coders among you: Powerpatcher [tonfilm](https://vvvv.org/people/tonfilm) has released [Stride SDSL Shader Tools](https://marketplace.visualstudio.com/items?itemName=tebjan.sdsl) which provides full IntelliSense for SDSL shaders in Visual Studio Code!

## Learning vvvv
Here some upcoming opportunities for learning vvvv:

- Februray 26th and 27th: [UI Frameworks & Patterns](https://thenodeinstitute.org/courses/ui-frameworks-patterns-in-vvvv/)

- Ready for use: [Interactive Sketches with VVVV](https://thenodeinstitute.org/courses/interactive-sketches-with-vvvv/) a collection of functional examples for interactive media projects.
