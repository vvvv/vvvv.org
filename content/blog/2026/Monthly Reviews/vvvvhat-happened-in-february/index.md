Spring!

...and here's a little update of things that are happening under the hood:
- Work on the already announced Package Manager is in full swing. Not much left to do for a public preview!
- Official support for Lasers is in the works, making it easier than ever to work with lasers from vvvv
- Boygrouping is going to see a comeback: vvvv's legendary client/server render cluster technology is coming to vvvv gamma. We're already testing in the field. If you have a project that could benefit from it, please [get in touch](mailto:devvvvs@vvvv.org)!

Coming up at The NODE Institute: The [33rd vvvvorldwide meetup](https://thenodeinstitute.org/event/33-vvvv-worldwide-meetup/) on March 19.
