### Crowd Tracking
	
For capturing interaction with crowds, vvvv supports the following tracking systems which give you individual peoples position (and more) in large spaces:
- [Augmenta](https://augmenta.tech/) 
- [BlueIOT](https://www.blueiot.com/)
  
Further, a range of depth sensors and lidars is supported that allow you to create custom solutions, see the [Devices section](/packs/?c=Devices#Depth%20Cameras) in the packs browser for all options.

### Multi Touch

Be it for [tabletop solutions](https://www.interactive-scape.com/), walls or floors using [Lidars](https://vvvv.org/packs/?c=Devices#Lidars), vvvv has all it takes to build touch solutions at any scale:
- [TUIO](http://tuio.org) - The standard protocol for talking to tangible multi touch surfaces
- [TrackObjects](https://www.nuget.org/packages/VL.TrackObjects) - To follow and identify objects over time and assigns them a stable and unique ID
